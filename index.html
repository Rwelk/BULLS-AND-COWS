This is a version of Bulls and Cows (some might know it as Mastermind) that I coded in a week. Sorry in advance for weird looking/clunky content.
There is one final secret code that your team must solve. You will have to guess the order of the five colored tokens. Note that this is unrelated to the order of the tokens needed to reach this website.

 To do this, select the order you think the colors must go in, and hit submit. I will tell you how many of the colors you got in the right position. Continue placing guesses until you get all five in the right order.

Be careful! There is a 30-second timer between submissions, so make your guesses carefully! Also, refreshing the page will create a new sequence and you'll lose all progress, so probably don't do that.
